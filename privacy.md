# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

LottoX ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy.

## Information We Collect

LottoX may collect:

- Anonymous analytics
- App crash reports
- Device information
- Subscription status via Apple App Store or Google Play
- Favourite lottery numbers (stored locally unless cloud sync is enabled)

We do NOT collect:

- Credit card information
- Lottery ticket purchases
- Government identification
- Personal betting history

Payments are processed securely by Apple App Store or Google Play.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Improve LottoX
- Provide Premium features
- Restore purchases
- Diagnose crashes
- Improve app performance

## Analytics

We may use services such as:

- RevenueCat
- Firebase Analytics
- Firebase Crashlytics

These services collect anonymous usage information.

## Third-Party Services

LottoX may use:

- Apple App Store
- Google Play
- RevenueCat
- Firebase

Each provider has its own Privacy Policy.

## Data Storage

Most lottery analysis is performed locally on your device.

Subscription information is securely managed by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat.

## Children's Privacy

LottoX is not intended for children under 18 years of age.

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time.
